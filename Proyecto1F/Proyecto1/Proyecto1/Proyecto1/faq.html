FAQ

How do you ship paintings?

We make use of FedEx premium service, which ensures a 2-day shipping to anywhere in the world.

What if something happens to my paintings during shipping?

FedEx premium service offers a complete cover warranty for your paintings.

Can I pick up my paintings personally?

Of course, you can pick up your paintings in our HQs, you will just need a valid ID.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept VISA, MasterCard, American Express and PayPal only. We do not accept cash.

Can I put my art in auction with your services?

Yes, we encourage artists to work with us to find a better price for their paintings.

What are your selling fees?

Currently we have a 10% service fee and 16% tax fee.

How can I know that my art is legit and not copied?

You will receive a document that certifies with total precision that the piece is original.
